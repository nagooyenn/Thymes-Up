Cheese Naan Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • Salt
  • 2 tablespoons sugar

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the butter and milk and microwave for 50 seconds.
  2. Add the active dry yeast and mix well. Add the salt and sugar and mix well.
  3. Fold in the bread flour and cake flour until fully incorporated and microwave for 20 seconds.
  4. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and divide it into 6 pieces. Take a piece and roll it into a ball. Roll it out to a round about ⅛ inch (3-mm) thick using a rolling pin. Repeat with the remaining dough.
  5. Put a third of the cheese into the center of the dough. Top with another dough round and crimp the edges to seal. Repeat with the remaining cheese and dough.
  6. Heat a medium nonstick frying pan over medium heat. Add a filled naan round and cook for 3 minutes, until lightly browned.
  7. Flip the naan bread, then use a spatula to flatten and cook for 3 minutes more. Cook the remaining naan.
  8. Slice into wedges and serve. Enjoy!

Fluffy Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of flour
  • 4 tablespoons baking powder
  • 4 cups milk
  • 3/4 cup butter

Directions

  1. Whisk together the flour and baking powder in a large bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, add the butter, milk, and egg yolks, stirring to combine.
  3. In a third bowl, use a hand mixer to beat the egg whites until soft peaks form.
  4. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture and stir with rubber spatula until just combined. Add the beaten egg whites and gently fold to combine.
  5. Heat an 8-inch (20cm) nonstick skillet over low heat. Pour 1 ½ cups of pancake batter into the skillet, smooth out the top, then cover with a lid. Cook until golden brown on the bottom and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 20 minutes. Transfer the pancake to a plate and repeat, making 3 more pancakes with the remaining batter.
  6. Serve with a toppping of your choice. Enjoy!

Scalloped Patatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 1 cup milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C).
  2. In a small pot, melt the butter and fry the garlic until it is just starting to brown. Add the flour, salt and pepper. whisk until there are no lumps.
  3. Slowly drizzle in the milk while constantly whisking to make sure the mixture is smooth.
  4. Bring to a boil, then remove from heat.
  5. Slice the potatoes into about ⅛-inch (3 mm) thick slices, then fan them out in a small baking dish.
  6. Pour the sauce on top of the potatoes, then sprinkle with parmesan.
  7. Bake for about 1 hour, until the top is bubbly and golden brown.
  8. Sprinkle chopped parsley on top, then serve. Enjoy!

Chopped Mediterranean Salad

Ingredients

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 red onion
  • 1/2 cup feta cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
  • 1 cup olives
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

  1. Peel the cucumbers. Cut into discs, and then quarter the discs. Place in a large salad bowl.
  2. Cut tomatoes into quarters, lengthwise, and then rotate and cut into large chunks. Add to the salad bowl.
  3. Cut the red onion in half, removing the skin, stem, and bottom. Thinly slice and add to the bowl.
  4. Add the feta cheese, olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper, lemon juice, and olives to the salad bowl and toss gently.
  5. Enjoy!

Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 14 oz chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Penne pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat until shimmering. Add the onion, carrots, celery, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until the vegetables are very soft, about 15 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the stock and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the noodles and cook for 6 minutes, then add the chicken and cook for about 2 minutes more, until the noodles are tender and the chicken is warmed through.
  4. Season to taste with more salt and pepper, then stir in the parsley.
  5. Enjoy!

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 large carrots
  • 4 stalks celery
  • Salt & Pepper
  • 4 cups chicken
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. In a pot, heat the oil and brown the chicken.
  2. Add garlic, and sauté for about a minute.
  3. Add broth, cream, and pasta, and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and reduce the heat to a simmer for 15-20 min.
  5. Turn off heat and stir in parmesan cheese.
  6. Top with parsley. Enjoy!
