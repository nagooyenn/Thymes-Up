Chicken Noodle Soup

One of the classics, chicken noodle soup. When your craving a warm and satisfying meal, this is the best pick for you.

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 large carrots
  • 4 stalks celery
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 8 oz egg noodles
  • 4 cups chicken
  • Parsley

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large soup pot over medium heat until shimmering. Add the onion, carrots, celery, salt, and pepper. Cook, stirring frequently, until the vegetables are very soft, about 15 minutes.
  2. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the stock and bring to a boil.
  3. Add the noodles and cook for 6 minutes, then add the chicken and cook for about 2 minutes more, until the noodles are tender and the chicken is warmed through.
  4. Season to taste with more salt and pepper, then stir in the parsley.
  5. Enjoy!
