Chicken Alfredo

A one-pot meal quick and easy but very satisfying for the busy week of yours. Topped off with parmesan cheese making it very creamy and delicious.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 14 oz chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Penne pasta
  • Parmesan cheese
  • parsley

Instructions:

  1. In a pot, heat the oil and brown the chicken.
  2. Add garlic, and sauté for about a minute.
  3. Add broth, cream, and pasta, and bring to a boil.
  4. Cover and reduce the heat to a simmer for 15-20 min.
  5. Turn off heat and stir in parmesan cheese.
  6. Top with parsley. Enjoy!
